Bhashyam Blooms

welcome!

Bhashyam Blooms offers education from Nursery to X standard and follows the CBSE pattern of education. It provides an opportunity for every child to think independently, ask questions and seek answers. The emphasis is on the overall development of the child ready to face the global challenges while holding on firmly to the traditional Indian values. Every child is talented and is always brimming with ideas. At Bhashyam these ideas are received and given due consideration. After all, who knows, there could be a Venki Ramakrishnan or an Abdul Kalam hiding within. Blooms offers the students an exclusive learning experience with global standards. It is the first multi cultural school in Guntur with a curriculum for learning with a difference.
